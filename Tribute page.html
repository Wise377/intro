Diogo Jota

1996 – 2025

Full name: Diogo José Teixeira da Silva
Born: 4 December 1996, Porto, Portugal
Died: 3 July 2025 (age 28 years), Cernadilla, Spain
Nationality: Portuguese
Parents: Joaquim Silva, Isabel Silva
Siblings: André Silva
Dates joined: 19 September 2020 (Liverpool FC)

Diogo Jota was a talented Portuguese footballer known for his skill, determination, and sportsmanship on and off the field. He played for clubs such as Wolverhampton Wanderers and Liverpool, as well as the Portugal national team. His passion for the game and his positive spirit inspired fans around the world.
